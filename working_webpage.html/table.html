A laptop is a portable personal computer featuring a clamshell design with a screen, keyboard, and trackpad, running desktop operating systems. It packs all standard computing components — display, speakers, and input devices — into a compact, battery-powered unit that can be folded for easy transport. Laptops serve a wide range of purposes, from work and education to gaming, content creation, and everyday home use, with hardware specs varying across different models and price ranges.
